# NOTTO Guidelines for Organ Donation

## Overview

The National Organ and Tissue Transplant Organization (NOTTO) is the apex body responsible for organ donation and transplantation in India under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.

## Organizational Structure

### NOTTO (National Level)
- Apex body for transplantation activities
- Maintains national registry and waiting list
- Coordinates inter-state organ sharing

### ROTTO (Regional Level)
- Regional Organ and Tissue Transplant Organization
- 5 regional bodies across India
- Coordinates within the region

### SOTTO (State Level)
- State Organ and Tissue Transplant Organization
- Manages state-level registrations
- Coordinates within the state

## Key Responsibilities

1. **Registration of Hospitals**: All transplant hospitals must register with NOTTO
2. **Waiting List Management**: Maintain fair and transparent waiting lists
3. **Organ Allocation**: Ensure equitable distribution based on medical criteria
4. **Data Management**: Collect and analyze transplantation data
5. **Training and Awareness**: Conduct training programs and public awareness campaigns

## Allocation Principles

1. **Medical Urgency**: Priority to critical patients
2. **Blood Type Compatibility**: Must match for successful transplant
3. **Waiting Time**: Longer waiting patients get priority
4. **Geographic Proximity**: Minimize organ transit time
5. **Age and Health Compatibility**: Match donor-recipient characteristics
